Kazoku (meaning "family" in Japanese) is a program we started in 2019 to help JSA members get closer to each other! It's hard for each and every member of JSA to attend every event, so we split our members into smaller Kazokus with a big/little system, hoping members can build stronger relationships. Our current program has approximately 150 members with an average family size of 12 members. These Kazokus do everything from karaoke to making snowmen in the Arb! If you are interested in joining, please fill out the form below!
