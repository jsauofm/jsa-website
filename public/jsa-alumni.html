MEET OUR ALUMNI


Over the years, our JSA members have accomplished a great deal during their post-graduate years. We have a growing alumni network who are based in Japan and the U.S. with a variety of jobs in tech, consulting, journalism, and more. Read more about our alumni and their favorite JSA memories below!
