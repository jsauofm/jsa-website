Our newsletter is a great way to look back on past events, get to know some of our general members, and learn a bit about Japanese culture. Our newsletter committee is comprised of Seiji Hoshino, who covers our events, Nami Kaneko, who provides fun games/recommendations, and Tiger Hashikura, who writes our Humans of JSA section. To get in touch with them, see their profile cards in the officers section on our home page.
