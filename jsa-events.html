PAST EVENTS


We often collaborate with other cultural orgs at the University of Michigan, universities in Japan, and other Midwestern JSAs each school year. We also have weekly coffee chats and other events which encourage community building on and off campus. Hover over each of the images below to check out some of JSA's events!


WINTER 2022


Game Night

With a variety of games including poker, euchre, and Super Smash Bros., our members got to engage in some friendly competition for prizes.









Winter Mass Meeting

Winter mass meeting is our way of updating our members on things to look forward to in the upcoming semester. It's an event where members can get to know new people in icebreaker games like Where the Wind Blows.









FALL 2021


Cookie Exchange

Members teamed up to prepare their best (or worst) cookie recipes for others to taste. Prizes were given out for the best looking, the tastiest, and the most interesting cookies. We also had some hot chocolate and a snowflake making station!









Talent Show

With incredible acts ranging from singing and playing guitar to yo-yoing to a rendition of the popular Get Down (ゲッダン) meme, our talent show brought a night that was filled laughs and applause.









Celebrasia

We participated in the Chinese Student Assocation's annual Celebrasia event. Sporting happi and hachimaki, we sold out of our onigiri within the first hour! There were lots of food, games, and performances from other cultural orgs there, too!









Asia's Got Spice

For our annual AGS event, we invited five other Asian orgs including AKDPhi, CSA, HKSA, VSA, and KSA to serve up some of their spiciest dishes. From JSA's mild and spicy curry rice to HKSA's sour and spicy noodles, there were many delicious foods from different cultures all in one place!









Halloween Movie Night

Members dressed up in costume and joined us in watching the Studio Ghibli film Spirited Away to celebrate Halloween.









MJSA

Our members traveled to Ohio State University for the Midwestern JSA conference where we met new people from other schools, practiced leadership and cultural awareness skills, and watched a traditional Japanese tea ceremony.
