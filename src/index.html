ABOUT JSA


The Japan Student Association (JSA) at the University of Michigan is a non-profit student organization open to students of any cultural or ethnic background, dedicated to promoting a welcoming environment for people to meet others who share their interests in Japanese culture. We hold general meetings as well as various social events, and provide career services by connecting students with recruiters from various Japanese companies, so if you are at all interested in Japanese culture, come check us out!
