ABOUT JSA


The Japan Student Association (JSA) at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor is a non-profit student organization dedicated to promoting a better campus understanding of the various aspects of Japan and the Japanese culture. JSA is primarily a social group and serves as a forum for people to meet others who share their interests in Japanese culture. We hold general meetings as well as various social events, and we provide career services by connecting students with recruiters from various Japanese companies. JSA is open to students of any cultural or ethnic background. If you are at all interested in Japanese culture, come check us out!
